Identity

Real Name: Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark Alter Ego: Iron Man

background

Tony Stark is a genius billionaire, industrialist, and philanthropist. He inherits Stark Industries from his father, Howard Stark.

Creation Of Ironman

Tony Stark creates the first Iron Man suit to escape captivity after being kidnapped by terrorists. The original suit is a bulky, gray armor powered by a miniaturized arc reactor.

Arc Reactor

The arc reactor is a clean energy source initially created to keep shrapnel from reaching his heart. It later powers the Iron Man suits.
